🧺 About KiondoNet

Weaving Kenya's mobile networks together

📖 Our Story

KiondoNet was born from a simple observation: millions of Kenyans waste airtime and bundles every month because they're on the "wrong" network. You have Safaricom airtime but need to call an Airtel number. Your friend has Airtel data but needs Safaricom. Your bundle expires tomorrow and you won't use it.

Existing solutions take 15-30% of your value. WhatsApp groups are unregulated and full of scams. There was no trusted, fair, and easy way to swap mobile value across networks.

So we built KiondoNet — Kenya's first peer-to-peer platform for swapping airtime, bundles, and M-Pesa across Safaricom, Airtel, and Telkom.

🧺 Why "KiondoNet"?

A Kiondo is a traditional handwoven basket — strong, flexible, and made to carry what matters. It represents community, trust, and connection.

KiondoNet weaves together Kenya's mobile networks into one connected basket where value flows freely between users.

"Weave your network."

To unlock the full value of every Kenyan's airtime and bundles.

No middlemen taking 30% cuts. No wasted expiring bundles. No scams. Just direct, trusted swaps between real people using M-Pesa escrow and WhatsApp.
