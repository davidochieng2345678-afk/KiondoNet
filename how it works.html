🔒 M-Pesa Escrow Protection

Every swap on KiondoNet is protected by M-Pesa escrow:

  • You pay a small 10 KES escrow fee when matched
  • Money is held safely until both parties confirm the swap
  • If something goes wrong, we investigate and refund
  • No more scams on WhatsApp groups
💡 Note: The 10 KES fee is refunded if the swap completes successfully. It only goes to KiondoNet if someone cancels unfairly.
